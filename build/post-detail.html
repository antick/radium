Why we should never ignore the food that we eat everyday

May 1, 2020 - 1 min read

For a long time, I have wanted to write this out. This is very important for us to understand. Eating good is important for our body and it shouldn't be ignored. We should give enough importance to the food that we eat everyday. Food is life!

Let me be very honest with you while I say this to you. I have been avoiding and losing my focus at my eating habits and that have caused a lot of issues. I had to focus back on my eating habits to avoid the problems. You should do the same. Never underestimate the power of food that you eat.

You become what you eat

This is very true. We should really focus on everything that is out there trying to teach us about food. Food is important and nobody in this entire world can say no to the food. We eat all our lives. Food is the source of our power and everything that we do. This just not only affect the body but also affects our mind at great extent.

Go ahead and ignore this advise and you shall experience the horror of health issues caused by wrong eating habits. Follow this and you shall live happily ever after.

This is no joke when it is about food. There are so many things in the world to eat and yet you have to kill the animals for your taste. But do you know that the animals do not taste at all. It is the spices that tickles your taste buds not the meat and fishes that you eat.

Don't tell us what to eat

Everybody is free to eat whatever they desire. You are no food police. Eat whatever you want but this is a curious thing that you want to eat meat which has no taste. I ate it too yet I see nothing wonderful about it. I can eat other things which tasts much better than a dead animal.

I would deliberately avoid eating the meat and fish. I find no taste and fun into that. Others might find all of these delicious but not me!
